Brand Refresh


The objective for the speculative design refresh of The Big Issue is to create a brand that embodies inclusivity and celebrates the inherent self-worth of its vendors. By infusing the brand with a new, modern design aesthetic, the goal is to not only enhance its visual appeal but also make it more accessible and engaging to a broader audience. This will elevate The Big Issue as not only a platform for social change but also as a beacon of hope within our communities.
