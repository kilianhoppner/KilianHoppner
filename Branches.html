Type Design


Branches is a typeface derived from the organic shapes and patterns found in Australia’s diverse landscapes. Drawing from my own photographs, I studied the structure of trees to create characters that embody nature's intricate forms. This process encourages a deeper connection to our environment and serves as a reminder that beauty exists all around us if we remain present.
